Good mental health is fundamental to proper functioning and quality of life.


It involves the ability to deal with stress, maintain healthy relationships, make decisions, deal with emotions and face life's challenges in a balanced way.

Mental health problems can affect anyone and are common across the world. Some examples of mental health conditions include anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), and many others.
It's important to seek professional help when needed, such as from psychologists, psychiatrists or therapists, if you or someone you know is experiencing mental health challenges.

Balanced and satisfying life

There are many strategies that can help promote it, such as: practicing meditation, relaxation, cognitive-behavioral therapy, seeking social support and promoting self-care.

Seek help

It is essential to be aware of your own needs and seek help when necessary.

Ups and downs are common

It is important to remember that mental health is not something static and can vary throughout life.
