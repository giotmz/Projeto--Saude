WHY DO PHYSICAL ACTIVITY

Doing physical activity is essential for health and well-being in many ways.

Reduced risk of chronic diseases

Regular physical activity is associated with a lower risk of developing chronic diseases such as type 2 diabetes, certain types of cancer and neurodegenerative diseases.

Improved physical conditioning

Regular exercise improves endurance, muscle strength, flexibility and coordination, making daily activities easier and more enjoyable.

Socialization

Participating in group physical activities or team sports can promote socialization, improve teamwork skills, and provide a sense of belonging.

Walking

Super democratic, walking is Brazilians' favorite physical activity, as it can be done by people of all ages, without major restrictions. Furthermore, you don't need any special equipment, it is one of the best options for outdoor physical activities and can be done anywhere.

Cycling

And as if it weren't enough to be an environmentally friendly transport that allows us to rediscover the city's spaces, the bicycle is also a powerful tool for health. In fact, cycling is a great ally, as it helps burn calories without causing major impacts on your joints.

Dance

For those who can't give up exercising in a fun way, then dancing is the right activity. This is because all styles bring benefits.

Bodybuilding

One of the best-known examples of physical activity. Since bodybuilding is a complete modality that works different muscle groups in a single workout, through different movements and exercises.
